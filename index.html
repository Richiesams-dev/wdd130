Edo

Edo, officially known as Edo State, is bordered by Kogi State to the North, Delta State to the East and South, and Ondo State to the West. Lying between latitude 5.440 and 7.340 North and longitude 5.40 and 6.450 East, the State has a land mass of 19,187 square km, is a state in the South-South geopolitical zone of the federal republic of Nigeria. As of 2024, the state was ranked as the 22nd most populous state in Nigeria.
